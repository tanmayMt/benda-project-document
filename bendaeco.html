User Types

Three Roles, One Platform

Each role has a tailored registration path and gets access to the products most relevant to them.

01   Job Seeker
Job Seeker
Individuals seeking employment who want to boost their resume, track applications, and improve their job readiness.
Registration Type
🔵 Personal email address
Products Accessible
🎯 ATS 📄 Resume Builder
  • Upload resume and receive ATS score
  • Get recommended courses based on ATS score
  • Resume improvement suggestions
  • Apply for jobs and track applications
  • Job assistance programs
02   Recruiter
Recruiter
Hiring professionals who post jobs, manage candidate pipelines, schedule interviews, and evaluate talent through assessments.
Registration Type
🟢 Business email (e.g. recruiter@company.com)
Products Accessible
🎯 ATS 📝 H-OR-G
  • Post jobs and manage applicant pipeline
  • Interview scheduling and candidate tracking
  • Create and assign tests via H-OR-G
  • View test scores and generate reports
  • Offer management and hiring workflow
03   Business
Business / Org
Companies and organizations managing their entire HR lifecycle from onboarding to payroll within a unified HRMS.
Registration Requirements
🟡 Company name · Business email · Location
Products Accessible
🏢 HRMS
  • Full employee lifecycle management
  • Attendance, leave & payroll modules
  • Performance management tools
  • Recruitment pipeline and onboarding
  • Document management
Business Registration

Domain Verification Logic

When a business registers, the system checks whether the email domain is already verified to streamline onboarding.

Existing Verified Domain

If the email domain is already registered and verified in the system, the organization is automatically validated — no manual review required.

hr@google.com → ✅ Auto-Verified
  • Instant access after registration
  • No admin approval needed
  • Domain recognized from existing org record
New Company Registration

If the domain does not exist in the system, the company must submit verification details. An admin reviews and approves the registration.

info@newstartup.io → 🕐 Pending
  • Company name required
  • Business email required
  • Location required
  • Admin reviews and approves

Authentication

Login Options & SSO

Users can sign in directly on any product or through the central Benda Infotech portal with Single Sign-On — no re-entry of credentials required.

Flexible Authentication
Two paths to access the platform. One seamless experience regardless of entry point.
Option A
Direct Product Login
Users can visit any product subdomain directly and log in independently. No central portal required.
  • ats.bendainfotech.com
  • resume.bendainfotech.com
  • horg.bendainfotech.com
  • hrms.bendainfotech.com
Option B
Central SSO Login
Login once through bendainfotech.com. Access any authorized product from the Unified Dashboard — no re-login needed.
After login → Unified Dashboard → Click product → Instantly opens with active session. No password prompt.

Architecture Summary

Platform Design Principles

The core architectural decisions that define how the Benda Infotech ecosystem is built and maintained.

Main Portal — Central entry point for all users
Central Authentication / Single Sign-On (SSO)
Independent product applications with own subdomains
Independent admin panels per product
Unified dashboard on Benda Infotech main portal
Direct login allowed on each individual product
Single master user database with product access control
Isolated business databases per product
Domain verification for business/organization onboarding
Role-based access control (RBAC) across all products
