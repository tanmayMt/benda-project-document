Security

Authentication & User Roles

Secure sign-in, registration, and role-based routing so each user only sees the tools and data they are allowed to use.

📧

Email/Password Auth

Register and login via /api/auth/register and /api/auth/login. JWT session stored client-side with header profile updates without page reload.

🛤️

Route Protection

Client-side role guards via useRequireRole and useStoredUser hooks redirect unauthorized users away from protected pages.

⚙️

Account Management

Password change, logout-all-devices, account deactivation, and full deletion — all available from candidate settings.

Candidate Features

Give job seekers a single place to search roles, manage profile and resumes, track applications, and respond to interviews and offers.

Routes /candidate /candidate/home /candidate/profile /candidate/settings /candidate/applications /candidate/interviews /candidate/offers
📋

Dashboard

Applications summary, recommendations, upcoming interviews, active offers, and a notifications preview — all in one view.

👤

Rich Profile

Name, skills, experience, education, certifications, portfolio links, and a visual profile completion percentage.

📝

Apply to Jobs

Apply with resume selection, skills, and optional cover letter. Withdraw any application before final hire or reject states.

🎯

Job Match Score

Skill and experience-based match percentage shown per job — helping candidates prioritize their strongest opportunities.

⭐

Company Reviews

Rate companies on culture and interview experience to help other candidates make informed decisions.

👁️

Profile Visibility

Choose from public, private, recruiters-only, or anonymous modes to control who sees your profile.

Example

A candidate completes their profile to 85%, applies to three jobs with their default resume, and tracks each application status from "Applied" through "Interview Round 1" on the application detail page.

Discovery

Job Search & Discovery

Help candidates find relevant published jobs and explore role details before applying.

Routes /candidate/home /candidate/jobs /candidate/jobs/[id] /candidate/saved-jobs /candidate/job-alerts /
🗺️

Browse & Search

Browse published jobs with keyword search and location filters. Header search available on both /home and /jobs.

Job Detail Pages

Full job details including title, description, required skills, salary range with currency, location, remote mode, and experience level.

Example

Candidate searches "React" + "Texas", opens a job card, reviews salary range in USD, and clicks Apply. An alert set for "Data Engineer" + "Remote" + weekly includes matching jobs in its next digest.

ATS Core

Applications

Core ATS record linking a candidate to a job — tracking status, score, notes, interviews, and offers across the full hiring lifecycle.

Routes /candidate/applications /candidate/applications/[id] /recruiter/applicants /admin/applications
📊

Application Fields

Status, stage, match score, priority, source, visa status, expected salary, assigned recruiter, hiring manager, and resume reference.

🕒

Status History

Full timeline of who changed status, when, and why — plus human-readable pipeline activity logs (stage moved, note added, assigned).

🔍

Recruiter Filters

Filter by job, status, minimum score, skills, and search by candidate name, email, or job title.

📝

Notes & Rating

Internal recruiter comments per application with a 0–5 candidate rating. Fully scoped and private.

⚡

Bulk Updates

Select multiple applications and bulk-update status or stage from both the applicant list and the Kanban pipeline.

🔒

Unique Constraint

One application per candidate per job — enforced at the database level to prevent duplicate submissions.

Example

After applying, the application enters Applied. The recruiter moves the candidate to Shortlisted on the pipeline — the candidate immediately sees "Shortlisted" on My Applications and receives a notification.

Drag & Drop Kanban

Powered by @dnd-kit — drag cards between columns and stage changes auto-save to the API with optimistic UI rollback.

🔎

Compact Toolbar

Job filter, search, sort, and collapsible advanced filters including recruiter, location, experience, match %, visa, source, priority, and date range.

📋

Rich Candidate Cards

Avatar, role, experience, location, salary, match %, badges, interview chips, and latest note preview — all at a glance.

⚡

Quick Actions

View profile, open resume drawer, schedule interview, add note, move stage, or reject — all from the card context menu.

📄

Resume Drawer

Inline PDF preview or download with skills, activity, and notes — view resumes without leaving the pipeline board.

📊

KPI Bar

Live totals for applicants, screening, interviews, offers, hired, rejected, time-to-hire, and conversion rate.

Example

Recruiter filters pipeline to "MERN Developer", drags a candidate from Applied → Interview Round 1. The system logs "Candidate moved from Applied to Interview Round 1 by Sarah HR" and notifies the candidate.

Scheduling

Interview Management

Schedule and track interviews between recruiters and candidates — with auto pipeline updates and status notifications.

Routes /recruiter/interviews /candidate/interviews /admin/interviews
📅

Schedule Interview

Set application, date/time, online or onsite format, meeting link, location, and notes — then send the invite.

✅

Candidate Confirmation

Candidates confirm attendance from their portal. Confirmation updates the interview status and notifies the recruiter.

⭐

Complete with Rating

Recruiters mark interviews complete and add a star rating (1–5) with notes — captured in the application history.

🔄

Auto Pipeline Move

Scheduling an interview automatically advances the candidate to the relevant interview stage via the application status service.

🏷️

Status Chips

Pipeline cards show real-time interview chips: Scheduled, Pending Feedback, Completed, and Cancelled.

Example

Recruiter schedules a Zoom interview for next Tuesday. Candidate confirms attendance. After the call, the recruiter marks it complete with a 4-star rating — notes are saved to the application record.

Closing

Offer Management

Release formal offer letters and track acceptance or decline — all the way through to hire.

Routes /recruiter/offers /candidate/offers Pipeline shortcuts
📃

Create Offer Letter

Define title, salary, joining date, and letter content. Send the offer and track status in real time.

✅

Accept or Decline

Candidate accepts → application moves to hired. Candidate declines → recruiter is notified immediately via in-app and email.

⚡

Pipeline Shortcuts

Generate offer, mark sent, mark accepted, set joining date, and move to hired — all without leaving the pipeline board.

Alerts

Notifications

Keep users informed about applications, pipeline changes, interviews, and offers with in-app alerts and optional email.

📬

In-App Feed

Notification feed with read/unread state, badge counts, and contextual redirects to related pages on click.

🏷️

Notification Types

Covers job_applied, status_changed, interview_scheduled, offer_released, and new_applicant.

📧

Email Notifications

Sends via notificationService when SMTP is configured. Candidate preferences control email and browser push flags.

⚡

Live Sync

Header dropdown and notifications page stay in sync via a custom browser event — no page reload required.

Example

Candidate receives "Application status updated — Interview Round 1". Clicking the notification opens their interviews page and clears the unread badge counter.
